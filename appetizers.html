Appetizers Menu

Here are some delicious appetizers to start your meal:

Parippu waday

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 sprig curry leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Preperation Steps:

  1. Soak chana dal for 2 hours and drain.
  2. Grind the dal with onions, green chilies, curry leaves, salt, and pepper to a coarse paste.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
  4. Shape the mixture into small patties and deep fry until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.

Fish cutlet

Ingredients:

  • 200g fish fillet, boiled and flaked
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying

Preparation Steps:

  1. Mix the flaked fish with onions, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Shape the mixture into small cutlets.
  3. Dip each cutlet in beaten egg and coat with bread crumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the cutlets until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with ketchup or chutney.
