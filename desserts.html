Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup melted butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, vanilla, and butter. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into the pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Cool before serving and dust with icing sugar if desired.

Fresh Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • ½ cup grapes
  • 1 orange, peeled and segmented
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Combine all fruits in a large mixing bowl.
  2. In a small cup, whisk honey and lemon juice together.
  3. Drizzle dressing over fruits and toss gently.
  4. Chill for 10 minutes before serving.
